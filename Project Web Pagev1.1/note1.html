How to Combat Zoom Fatigue.

  • 1. Avoid Multitasking.

    Doing multiple things at once cuts into performance. While working on PC, close any tabs or programs that might distract you, put your phone away, and stay focused.

  • 2. Build in Breaks.

    Take mini breaks from Zoom calls by minimazing the window, or just looking away from your computer completely for a few seconds now and then. In between Zoom calls get up and move around for a bit.

  • 3. Reduce on screen stimuli.

    Hide yourself from view. Most people on video focus on other's people backgrounds, plants, furniture, bookshelfs. Using plain background helps to comb mental fatigue.

  • 4. Reduce on screen time.

    Check your schedule, if you can avoid Zoom calls, do it. Try to schedule Zoom calls/meetings/classes, for no more than 6 hours a day.
